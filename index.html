About Me

Adrianna Munoz is a passionate, hard charging and energetic Front-end Web Developer. She loves to challenge herself through learning new things, taking on new projects, and going the extra mile. This is evident in her thoughtful and competitive approach. She is fueled by her passion to tap into her creative mindset and solve problems for people. She considers herself a student for life and loves to sharpen her skills through learning. Weather that is staying in tune with the latest technology or collaborating with peers, she strides for an opportunity to continue her professional development. Adrianna believes mindfulness and being kind are keys to success, a tenet she lives out through her interests in hiking, working out, gardening, and playing with her Dog bff Athena. Adrianna is currently looking for work as a Front-end Web Developer and is always interested in a challenge. Reach out to munoz022098@gmail.com to connect!
